JQuery

    Was a helper for the browser that was once extremeley popular. It has falled out of favor for other frameworks like React, Bootstrap, and or other complex options for building websites. It is still a major part of the web and will be encountered in the wild many, many times over. JavaScript in the browser without any help or tools is considered vanilla JS. The base language of JS.

    Jquery takes the DOM and wraps the DOM into a helper toolset. Takes the complexity of the DOM and makes it easier to handle.
